Bio

About Me

I believe a great project starts with clear goals and thoughtful planning but evolves with the flexibility to adapt and solve challenges along the way.

Whether leading cross-functional teams or managing high-stakes projects, I thrive on collaboration, staying organized, and delivering exceptional results on time and within budget. I’m known for my ability to foster diverse, high-performing teams, carry a positive culture, and always keep an eye on downstream risks and opportunities. Whether it’s building a narrative from scratch or fine-tuning a big idea, I bring energy, focus, and creativity to every project I touch.

Experience

I am a Multimedia Producer and Director with a knack for turning complex ideas into engaging content that connects with audiences and elevates brand messaging.

My experience spans everything from live-streams to studio and location shoots. I have worked across healthcare, finance, tech, and more, managing everything from live-action documentaries to corporate campaigns. I have mastered the art of balancing big-picture strategy with the nitty-gritty details to keep projects on track and teams energized.

Skills

Video Production & Post-Production: Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, studio and on-location production.

  • Digital Strategy: Metadata creation, SEO, and analytics-driven refinements.
  • Directing Talent: Prepping, coaching and directing talent, from actors to C-suite executives.
  • Collaborative Leadership: Team scaling, talent coaching, vendor oversight.
  • Global Storytelling: Expertise in culturally adaptive content creation for diverse audiences.
