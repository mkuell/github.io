About

Behind the Work

A Creative Producer and Director balancing big-picture strategy with the details that keep projects on track.

I am a Multimedia Producer and Director with a knack for turning complex ideas into engaging content that connects with audiences and elevates brand messaging. My experience spans live-streams, studio shoots, and location production across healthcare, finance, education, and mission-driven campaigns—balancing creative quality with practical constraints.

A great project starts with clear goals and thoughtful planning, then evolves with the flexibility to adapt and solve challenges along the way. Whether leading cross-functional teams or managing high-stakes projects, I thrive on collaboration, clear communication, and reliable execution from pre-production through final delivery.

  • Directing Talent Prepping, coaching, and directing talent from actors to C-suite executives.
  • Collaborative Leadership Team scaling, talent coaching, and vendor oversight.
  • Digital Strategy Metadata creation, SEO, and analytics-driven refinement.
