About Me

I believe a great project starts with clear goals and thoughtful planning but evolves with the flexibility to adapt and solve challenges along the way. Whether leading cross-functional teams or managing high-stakes projects, I thrive on collaboration, staying organized, and delivering exceptional results on time and within budget. I’m known for my ability to foster diverse, high-performing teams, carry a positive culture, and always keep an eye on downstream risks and opportunities.

Experience

I am a Multimedia Producer and Director with a knack for turning complex ideas into engaging content that connects with audiences and elevates brand messaging. My experience spans everything from live-streams to studio and location shoots. Whether it’s building a narrative from scratch or fine-tuning a big idea, I bring energy, focus, and creativity to every project I touch.

Key Strengths

  • Creative Development: Ideation, scripting, and storyboarding.
  • Video Production: Multi-camera shoots and post-production.

  • Directing Talent: Prepping, coaching and directing actors to C-suite.
